Refund Policy

Every engagement begins with a written scope, acceptance criteria, fixed price, and delivery window. Paddle acts as merchant of record and processes approved refunds to the original payment method.

Before work begins

A buyer may cancel for a full refund before the Operator begins the agreed task. If the Operator determines after payment that the confirmed task cannot be performed lawfully or within the written scope, the buyer receives a full refund.

After delivery

Report a claimed failure against the written acceptance criteria within seven calendar days of delivery. The Operator will first reproduce the issue and provide a correction when the defect is within the agreed scope. If the agreed acceptance criteria remain unmet after that correction opportunity, the buyer may receive a full or proportionate refund based on the undelivered portion.

Excluded refund reasons

Refunds are not promised for a changed task, a new environment, buyer materials that differ from the confirmed scope, unavailable third-party systems, or dissatisfaction with a technically correct finding. This policy does not limit mandatory consumer rights that apply by law.

Requesting a refund

Email andudyun0504@gmail.com with the Paddle transaction or invoice identifier, the agreed acceptance criterion, and the evidence of the claimed failure. Do not send card or bank details.
